Our Community Tradition

Lace up for fun and fitness at the 3rd Annual Rock Dam Days 5K Run/Walk! Whether you're a competitive runner or looking for a scenic morning stroll with the family, this community event has something for everyone.

All proceeds benefit the Rock Dam Lake Association, supporting conservation and community efforts to keep our lake and surrounding area thriving for generations to come.

Walkers, joggers, and runners of all ages and abilities are welcome. Bring the family, enjoy the morning, and support a great cause!

On-Course Support

Water and snacks will be provided at the start/finish line for all participants. Multiple water stations along the course, plus volunteers in golf carts driving the route to offer support and water. You're never far from help!

Every Finisher Gets a Medal

Cross that finish line and take home a custom Rock Dam Days finisher medal. This year's design features the Wisconsin state outline with pine trees and the Rock Dam Days logo. Plus, prizes for the top 3 overall finishers!

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I walk instead of run? expand_more
Yes! This is a 5K Run/Walk. Walkers of all paces are welcome and encouraged.
Is this a timed race? expand_more
No. This is an unsanctioned, untimed, fun run event. No official times will be recorded or provided. We do our best to map out the course and get as close to 3.1 miles as possible, but the course may be slightly shorter or longer.
Do I get a t-shirt? expand_more
Yes! Your $20 registration includes a t-shirt with the 2026 Rock Dam Days logo. Register by June 15, 2026 to guarantee your size. After that date, shirts are first-come, first-served.
Can I register on race day? expand_more
Yes! Day-of registration is available from 6:45 - 7:45 AM at AKA Lakeside. Cash or Venmo accepted. Just note that t-shirt sizes may be limited for day-of registrants.
How do I pay? expand_more
Venmo: Send $20 to @jomegs (Megan Johnson).
Cash: Pay in person at packet pick-up (Friday evening or Saturday morning).
Will there be water on the course? expand_more
Yes! Water and snacks at the start/finish line, multiple water stations on the course, and volunteers in golf carts driving the route to offer water and support.
Are there prizes? expand_more
Every finisher receives a medal! Plus, prizes are awarded to the top 3 overall finishers.
Where does the money go? expand_more
All proceeds benefit the Rock Dam Lake Association, which supports lake conservation, water quality, and community initiatives in the Rock Dam Lake area.
